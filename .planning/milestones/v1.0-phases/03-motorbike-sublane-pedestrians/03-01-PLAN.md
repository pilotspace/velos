   - `lateral_gap_at(probe_lateral, neighbors, params) -> f64`: compute available lateral gap at a probe position considering all neighbor half-widths
   - `find_largest_gap(own_lateral, road_width, neighbors, params) -> f64`: scan road width for largest gap (used during swarming)
